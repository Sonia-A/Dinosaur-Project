About Dinosaur

Dinosaur Collection is the product of a passion for fashion and a firm belief that fashion is indeed for all. The mother of this result/company is Muhoza Pophia, a young entrepreneur from Rwanda. Dinosaur Collection was born in 2019. This brand is committed to making fashion accessible to all and to helping our clients discover, explore and find empowerment in their own styles. We revamp old clothes and we make personalized clothes for you, using our Ankara fabric which is rich in patterns and colors. We also collect clothes you no longer wear, to be donated to less privileged groups of our communities. Thank you for joining us in this journey. We believe that together,we can use fashion to manifest happy realities for each one of us all, because when you look good, you feel good!
