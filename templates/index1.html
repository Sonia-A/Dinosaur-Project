Fashion Charity

How it Works

  • Outdated Fashion?

    Trends come and go, but fashion is eternal. At Dinosaur collection, we believe what doesn’t make you happy anymore can spark somebody else’s joy. Reach out to us if you have old styles to donate or revamp.
    WE'VE GOT YOU!

  • Wardrobe MakeOver

    Just because you outgrew a certain style, does not mean that a new one cannot be created from the same clothes.If you have old material and new ideas, us, at Dinosaur Collection, we will enjoy bringing your ideas to life. Your old dress can become your new top, perhaps with a new kitenge touch too?
    TOGETHER, LET'S CREATE!

  • Family Affair

    Fashion is personal. Fashion is caring. Fashion is intentional; just like family. How would your little ones look in your clothes, were they to get adjusted to their sizes?
    WE WILL HELP YOU FIND OUT!

  • Fashion All

    At Dinosaur Collection,fashion is for all. We also recognize the state of wellness that comes with looking good. This is why we will be donating clothes to different underprivileged groups of our communities, as often as possible. We need your help here. With your generosity paired with your sense of and love for fashion, we can contribute to people’s joy, by donating clothes. Looking good make us all feel good.
    FEELING GOOD GETS US READY FOR THE WORLD!
